La Tania

One of France's best kept skiing secrets, La Tania lies between Courchevel and Meribel in the extensive Three Valleys ski area.
Purpose-built in 1992 for the Winter Olympics, this pretty little resort has been sympathetically constructed using natural materials.
A free bus service links La Tania with Courchevel 1850 during the day, although skiers will prefer
to take the gondola straight up to the ski area.


A quieter alternative to the better-known resorts in the
Three Valleys that’s ideal for families.


A perfect location between the resorts of Courchevel and Meribel,
with Ski-in, ski-out accommodation close to all the resort’s bars, restaurants and shops.
